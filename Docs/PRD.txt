# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# AI-Powered Citizen Question Intelligence Platform for West Bengal

**Version:** 1.0 (MVP)

**Document Owner:** Product Team

**Status:** Draft

---

# 1. Product Overview

## Vision

Build an AI-powered civic intelligence platform that enables residents of West Bengal to submit questions regarding public services, government schemes, infrastructure, healthcare, education, employment, and other governance-related topics. Instead of functioning as a grievance redressal portal, the platform collects citizen questions, applies Artificial Intelligence to identify recurring themes and trends, and generates structured reports for government officials.

The platform transforms thousands of unstructured citizen questions into concise, actionable intelligence that helps policymakers understand what information citizens need the most.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Government departments receive thousands of questions from citizens through multiple channels such as social media, local offices, public meetings, emails, and helplines.

These questions are:

* Scattered across multiple channels
* Difficult to analyze at scale
* Repetitive
* Written in multiple languages
* Often expressed using informal regional dialects
* Time-consuming to manually review

As a result:

* Officials cannot easily identify the most common citizen concerns.
* Communication gaps remain unresolved.
* Frequently asked questions remain unanswered publicly.
* Decision-makers lack statewide visibility into emerging public information needs.

---

# 3. Proposed Solution

The platform provides a centralized portal where any resident of West Bengal can submit governance-related questions.

Instead of manually reviewing every submission, an AI engine:

* Detects language
* Understands regional Bengali writing styles
* Groups similar questions
* Removes duplicates
* Identifies frequently asked topics
* Detects trends
* Generates executive summaries
* Produces scheduled reports for government officials

The platform focuses solely on **question intelligence** and does not replace existing grievance or complaint portals.

# 17. Risks

## Technical Risks

* AI misclassification
* Bengali dialect variations

* Email delivery failures

---

* Spam submissions
* Duplicate questions

* AI moderation
* Rate limiting

# 18. Version 1 Exclusions

* OTP verification

* Grievance resolution
* File uploads
* Audio submissions
* Video submissions
* Image submissions
* Public commenting
* Public voting
* Social sharing
* Notifications to citizens
* Live chat support
* Native Android/iOS applications
* Multi-state support
* Anonymous public dashboards
* Integration with government grievance systems

These features may be considered in future releases based on stakeholder feedback and adoption.

# 20. Product Success Statement

The success of this platform will not be measured by the number of questions collected alone, but by its ability to transform thousands of fragmented citizen questions into structured, multilingual, AI-generated intelligence that enables government officials to better understand public information needs, improve communication strategies, and make more informed governance decisions.
